In the modern developing world, automobile plays an important role especially two-wheeler i.e. (motorcycles& bikes) plays a major role. Even though they are helpful there are some accidents due to the carelessness of the ride

Published at the International Conference on Mechanical Engineering and Renewable Energy 2021
Design and Fabrication of a 360° Rotating Smart Table Fan with IoT Based Control System

This paper discusses a 360° rotation smart table fan with IoT based control system. This fan can rotate between 0° and 360° at the user's request, and it can be controlled using an Android mobile device from a distance. It can locate the human and then fix its position such that air is directed in that direction

Submitted for publication(Abstract Accepted at ICMIME 2022)
Object Avoiding Self Balancing Robot Using Ultrasonic Sensor

This paper presents a self-balancing robot that operates according to the inverted pendulum principle. It is a two wheeled vehicle that positions itself vertically in relation to the ground. The mechanical model is dependent on the structure of the cart, and pendulum system. A nonexclusive feedback controller (PID controller) is used to achieve its stable inverted position.

Submitted for publication(Abstract Accepted at ICMIME 2022)
Design and Fabrication of an Automotive Electromagnetic Braking System

This paper mainly focuses on the analysis and design of a 3D electromagnetic braking model capable of applying brakes without any friction loss and without losing the energy supplied. Because of the size and poor power-to-weight ratio, the traditional braking system is sometimes cumbersome. This method incorporates electromechanical principles.

Accepted for publication at STI'2022. Will be featured in IEEE Explore Digital Library)
Power Generation of Vertical Axis Wind Turbine using Magnetic Levitation

This study uses magnetic levitation technology to inaugurate a vertical axis wind turbine to improve performance. The blades are levitated from the base by magnets, which minimizes friction between the blades and the bottom. So the energy loss during rotation is minimized, improving the turbine's overall efficiency.

Submitted for publication(Abstract Accepted at ICRPSET 2022)
